Feature Banners

The feature banner component is overloaded IMO, but let us define as follows:

  • a container
  • topical callout
  • may or may not contain accompanying media assets (video link, image) Foreground and Background
  • see Bryan's draft CIBC_Banner_Explanation.pdf for more specification

The feature banner component is composed (at minimum) of the following:

Hug the Bottom

The image component should hug the right-hand side of this feature banner.

This is working if the impact *splat* of the ball is snug to the feature banner border in non-mobile viewports.

  • space to
  • see vertical
